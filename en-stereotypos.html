STEREOTYPOS

A unique experience paying tribute to Soda Stereo and Gustavo Cerati, characterized by a lively, authentic, and powerful celebration of the musical legacy of one of the most iconic Spanish-language rock bands. With hundreds of performances in cities such as Los Angeles, Las Vegas, Salt Lake City, and Mendoza, Argentina, STEREOTYPOS brings the energy and magic of Soda Stereo to every stage, rekindling the passion of multiple generations.

BIOGRAPHY

Founded in October 2022 in Los Angeles, California, STEREOTYPOS is a musical and artistic tribute to Soda Stereo and Gustavo Cerati. Led by Gonzalo Baumgartner, a musician from Mendoza living in the United States since 2001, the band seeks to convey the unique essence, energy, and style that made Cerati a reference in Argentine and Latin American music. Through its performances, STEREOTYPOS connects with audiences by passionately and respectfully recreating songs that have marked generations, keeping a legacy alive that continues to inspire listeners of all ages.

Origins and Vision

The project was born from the desire to revive the original energy of Soda Stereo live, combining sonic fidelity with a modern and emotional stage presentation.
